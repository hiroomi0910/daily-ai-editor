# Editorial Article Prompt

Given today's activity logs:

- Bluesky posts
- Twitter timeline
- GitHub activity
- Obsidian updates

Determine:

- What themes the user was thinking about today
- What conceptual continuity exists

Generate:

- 500-800 word quiet editorial article
- Markdown format
- Natural Japanese
- Reflective tone
- Not diary-style
- Not SNS summary

Phase 1 uses Bluesky posts only. Other input sources are future work.
